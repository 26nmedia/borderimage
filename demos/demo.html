borderImage

In the dark ages of table layouts, web pioneers used to build their whole pages using heavy, unmaintainable, innaccessible <table>s.

Then came css to relieve all designers with this simple promise: Allowing a clear separation between the content and the layout. But it failed to save them from slicing images with their precious hands and countless, meaningless <div>s to please our eyes.

Introducing CSS3 and the border-image property, your personnal design ninja. It is now possible to decorate any container, button, element of a page independently of it's position and dimensions in a flash.
Unleash your creativity, just design and describe how you would have sliced; sit back and let a ninja do the dirty job.
